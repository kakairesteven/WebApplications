True worth is in being, not seeming. In doing each day that goes by some little good.
Not in dreaming of great and great things to do by and by.
Whatsoever men think in their blindness, despite the follies of their youth,
there is nothing so kingly as loyalty and nothing so kind as truth.

A leader stands at the shores and sees a land no one else sees.

We are creators. we are change-makers. We are ordained with inherent powers of creativity by the ultimate creator who trusts in our powers to craft a new and better future. We make society better because that is what we are. We are Mak-ers.

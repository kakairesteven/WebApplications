She is crazy

JT Coldfire has played professionally for over 15 of his 31 years from Texas to New York and across the pond in Europe. Throughout his career, JT has gained the reputation as one of the hardest working musicians in Austin, sometimes playing 3 shows in a single evening never repeating the same song twice for over 9 hours of live performance. His travels all over the Texas Gulf Coast, Oklahoma, Tennessee, New York and as far off as Sweden, have gained an audience that is as dedicated to his performances as he is delivering them.
